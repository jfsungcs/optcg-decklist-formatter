OPTCG Decklist Formatter

How to Use

Paste your OPTCG deck list in the box below and click Convert to transform it into an array format.

After conversion, click Copy to Clipboard to copy the output.

Accepted Formats

✅ [multiplier]x[code] | 2x0P01-001 ... ✅ [multiplier]x[code] [card name] | 2x0P01-001 Zoro ... ✅ [multiplier] [code] [card name] | 2 0P01-001 Zoro ... ✅ [multiplier] [code] | 2 0P01-001 ...
